Escape from Drury Lane



Do you know the muffin men? I do. I do all too well... They have captured me again, but this time something seems different. Ah! They are zombies!! I need to escape. I see a door off in the distance, maybe it leads out of here. I will run run run as fast as I can but it might not be enough. Maybe I can use their own muffins against themselves. I am the gingerbread man. This is my life now.
